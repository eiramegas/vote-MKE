Why is it important to vote?

Local government manages a lot of different things — and their decisions will directly affect your life. There is no level of government that is more directly responsible for serving your community than your local elected officials. Local government can affect almost every aspect of your daily life.

Here’s just a few of the things they’re responsible for:

  • Local School Quality
  • Policing and Public Safety
  • Rent Costs and affordable Housing
  • Public Transportation
  • Deciding Sanctuary Juristiction
  • Alcohol and Marijuana ordinances
  • Recycling Options
  • And the list goes on and on...
